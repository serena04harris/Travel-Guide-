Explore Ireland

Discover the charm of Ireland with its rolling green hills, ancient castles, and vibrant traditions. Adventure and history await in the Emerald Isle.

Must See Attractions

Cliffs of Moher

Marvel at these iconic sea cliffs, offering breathtaking views of the Atlantic Ocean.

Dublin Castle

Explore Ireland's rich history at this historic castle in the heart of Dublin.

Ring of Kerry

Drive along this scenic route featuring picturesque landscapes and charming villages.

Getting Around Ireland

Rental Cars

Renting a car is the best way to explore Ireland's countryside and remote attractions.

Trains and Buses

Enjoy affordable and convenient travel options between major cities and towns.

Guided Tours

Join guided tours for a hassle-free way to visit popular destinations and learn about Irish history.

Things to Know

Drive on the left-hand side and plan your route ahead to avoid narrow rural roads.

Local Climate & Best Times to Visit

Ireland enjoys a temperate climate with mild temperatures year-round. The best time to visit is from May to September for pleasant weather and long daylight hours.

Important Information Before You Go

Travel Documents

Ensure your passport is valid. Visitors from certain countries may require a visa.

Health and Safety

Pack waterproof clothing and sturdy footwear for Ireland's unpredictable weather.

Currency

Ireland uses the Euro. Credit cards are widely accepted, but carry some cash for smaller establishments.

Travel Insurance

Consider travel insurance to cover unexpected events, such as medical emergencies or trip cancellations.
