Explore the Alps

Discover the majestic beauty of the Alps with world-class ski resorts, breathtaking snow-capped peaks, and unparalleled winter adventures.

Must See Ski Resorts

Chamonix

Ski in the legendary resort of Chamonix, one of the oldest and most renowned alpine destinations.

Zermatt

Enjoy skiing with views of the iconic Matterhorn, and experience one of the highest ski resorts in Europe.

St. Anton

Known for its challenging slopes and après-ski scene, St. Anton is a must-visit for seasoned skiers.

Getting Around the Alps

Ski Lifts

Most resorts offer extensive ski lift systems, providing easy access to all areas of the mountain.

Snowmobiles

Snowmobiles are a fun way to explore off-piste areas or get to more remote ski locations.

Local Tours

Experience guided skiing and snowboarding tours through the powder, or take a scenic snowshoeing excursion.

Things to Know

Prepare for high-altitude skiing, and always check weather conditions before venturing out into the mountains.

Local Climate & Best Times to Visit

The best time to ski in the Alps is from December to March, with the peak of the season being in January and February for the best snow conditions.

Important Information Before You Go

Ski Passes

Purchase a multi-resort ski pass to enjoy access to various ski resorts across the Alps.

Safety Tips

Always wear a helmet and appropriate gear, and never ski alone on unfamiliar slopes.

Equipment Rental

Ski equipment is available for rent at most resorts, including skis, snowboards, and snow gear.

Local Resources

Most resorts have ski schools and rental services for all levels of skiers. Visit the ski resort’s visitor center for helpful advice.
