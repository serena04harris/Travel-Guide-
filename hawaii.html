Explore Hawaii

Discover the magic of Hawaii with its pristine beaches, lush tropical landscapes, and vibrant culture. Adventure and relaxation await on these breathtaking islands.

Must See Attractions

Waikiki Beach

Enjoy the world-famous beach known for its golden sands, clear waters, and stunning sunsets.

Haleakalā National Park

Witness a breathtaking sunrise from the summit of this dormant volcano on Maui.

Nā Pali Coast

Explore this stunning coastline on Kauai, famous for its dramatic cliffs and lush greenery.

Getting Around Hawaii

Rental Cars

Renting a car is the most convenient way to explore the islands at your own pace.

Inter-Island Flights

Travel between islands quickly with frequent inter-island flights.

Public Transportation

Utilize local buses for affordable travel on Oahu and other islands.

Things to Know

Plan ahead for peak travel times, and book transportation and activities in advance.

Local Climate & Best Times to Visit

Hawaii enjoys a tropical climate year-round. The best time to visit is during the dry season (April to October) for sunny days and calm waters. The wet season (November to March) offers cooler temperatures and lush landscapes.

Important Information Before You Go

Travel Documents

US citizens don’t need a passport to visit Hawaii. International visitors should check entry requirements.

Health and Safety

Pack sunscreen and stay hydrated to enjoy Hawaii’s sunny weather safely.

Currency

Hawaii uses the US dollar. Credit cards are widely accepted, but carrying some cash is recommended for smaller vendors.

Travel Insurance

Consider travel insurance to cover unexpected events, such as medical emergencies or trip cancellations.
